Rock Paper Scissors, but a little different

How to Play

Behold, your opponents hand! What will you do?

In this game, your aim is not always to "win".

Each round you must aim to satisfy the condition marked on each card. Here, your opponent is playing ROCK. What hand must you play to to lose?

So in this round, your aim is to lose. Rock beats paper, so you must play paper to win.

So, sometimes in this game, to win is to lose... or win.. or tie... or lose...
