Rock Paper Scissors, but a little different

How to Play

Behold, your opponents hand! What will you do?

In this game, your aim is not always to "win".

Each round you must aim to satisfy the condition marked on each card. Here, your opponent is playing ROCK and your condition is to LOSE. What hand must you play to to lose?

Paper will lose against rock, so here you must play paper.

So, sometimes in this game, to win... is to lose...
